Chicken Parm

Description

This chicken parm recipe is an old family recipe, passed down from my father. He told me this recipe over the phone, and I could tell he was giving me measurements off the top of his head.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Pasta sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat eggs until well-mixed.
  2. Fill another bowl with the bread crumbs.
  3. Dredge chicken through eggs, then bread crumbs, then place into a pan with melted butter.
  4. Cook, flipping each four minutes. The last time you flip, add some mozzarella cheese and pasta sauce.
  5. Serve on a bed of spaghetti.
