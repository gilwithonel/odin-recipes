Scrambled Eggs

Description

These are scrambled eggs the way my father-in-law makes them. They are some of the best I've ever had.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Saute chopped onions thoroughly in a pan with melted butter.
  2. Turn burner down, then add whisked eggs.
  3. Season eggs with salt and pepper, then cook until done.
  4. Serve with hot sauce.
