Chicken Caprese

Description

Chicken Caprese will delight the mouth and the stomach. Using fresh mozzarella and cherry tomatoes gives this dish a freshness that could only be matched by Italy herself.

Ingredients

  • balsamic vinegar
  • honey
  • chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper
  • EVOO
  • red onion
  • etc

Steps

  1. In small saucepan, combine balsamic and honey. Heat and let reduce.
  2. Tenderize chicken, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add to oiled, heated pan and cook until done, then remove from pan.
  4. Add red onion and saute until soft, then add garlic, tomatoes and additional seasonings
  5. Return chicken to pan, then add mozzarella and let the cheese melt.
